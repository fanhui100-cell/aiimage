## 听力理解 · Listening（25 分，15 题/项）

**第 1 篇**

> Mom, can we make fruit ice pops? Sure! First, cut some strawberries and kiwis into small pieces. Then, put them into the ice pop molds. Next, pour some yogurt over the fruit. Finally, put the molds in the freezer for four hours. Yummy and healthy!

**1.** What can we guess about the speaker?
   A. She is a professional chef.   B. She works in a yogurt factory.   C. She is a child talking to her mom.   D. She dislikes cold food.
   ✅ C · 推断题：开头叫妈妈，语气像孩子，故选C。

**第 2 篇**

> Amy woke up with a sore throat and a high fever. Her mom took her to the clinic. The doctor checked her temperature and said, "You have a bad cold. Drink warm water and rest for two days." Then he gave her some medicine. Amy felt worried about the school test, but the doctor smiled and said, "Don't worry. Health comes first."

**2.** Why did Amy feel worried?
   A. She had a school test.   B. She didn't like the doctor.   C. She had a high fever.   D. She lost her medicine.
   ✅ A · 文中提到Amy worried about the school test，推断题。

**第 3 篇**

> Mr. Lee: Good morning, class. Why is everyone so quiet today?
Student: We have a surprise for you, Mr. Lee. It’s Teacher’s Day tomorrow, but you are going on a trip today.
Mr. Lee: Oh, you remembered! Thank you. What is it?
Student: We made a card and bought your favorite chocolate.
Mr. Lee: That’s very kind. I will miss you all.

**3.** What is the main topic of this conversation?
   A. A surprise for Teacher's Day.   B. A student's birthday party.   C. A trip plan for the class.   D. A chocolate tasting activity.
   ✅ A · 对话围绕学生提前为教师节给老师惊喜展开。

**第 4 篇**

> I found a blue water bottle on the bench near the playground. It has a cute cat sticker on it. I left it at the park office. If it is yours, please come and get it before 5 p.m. today. My name is Tom, and you can ask the worker there for me.

**4.** What is the passage mainly about?
   A. A worker at the park office.   B. A lost cat in the park.   C. A new playground for children.   D. A found water bottle with a sticker.
   ✅ D · 主旨：短文主要讲捡到一个带贴纸的水壶并告知失物招领。
**5.** What can we infer about the water bottle?
   A. The park office is closed all day.   B. It is very old and broken.   C. It belongs to a child who likes cats.   D. Tom will keep it for himself.
   ✅ C · 推断：有猫咪贴纸，推测主人可能喜欢猫，通常是孩子。

**第 5 篇**

> This morning, I took a shared bike to school. The roads were busy, but bike lanes were clear. Suddenly, a new electric bus passed by quietly. It didn't make any noise. I think green travel is becoming more popular in our city.

**6.** How did the speaker go to school this morning?
   A. By electric bus   B. By shared bike   C. By car   D. On foot
   ✅ B · 第一句明确提到‘I took a shared bike to school’。

**第 6 篇**

> Good morning, class. Today I want to share something cool. Did you know that trees can talk to each other? Under the ground, there is a network of fungi. It connects tree roots. When a tree needs water, other trees send some through this network. It's like a secret internet!

**7.** What is the main topic of this talk?
   A. How trees grow leaves.   B. How fungi harm trees.   C. How trees communicate with each other.   D. How to plant a tree.
   ✅ C · 主旨题。讲座主要讲树木通过真菌网络交流，故选B。

**第 7 篇**

> Tom is moving to a new apartment next week. The old one is too small and noisy. He found a bigger one near the park. It has two bedrooms and a nice kitchen. The rent is $800 a month, including water and gas. Tom is happy because he can walk to work now. He will buy new furniture for the living room this weekend.

**8.** What is the main reason Tom is moving?
   A. The old apartment is too small and noisy.   B. He wants a cheaper apartment.   C. He likes the park near his office.   D. The new apartment has a bigger kitchen.
   ✅ A · 原文明确说旧公寓太小且吵闹，是搬家主要原因。

**第 8 篇**

> Mom is out shopping. Dad is fixing a shelf in the living room. He asks me to hold the hammer. I pass it to him, but I accidentally drop the nails. Dad says, "No problem, let's pick them up together." Then we finish fixing the shelf. Mom comes back and says, "Good job!"

**9.** What is the main topic of the passage?
   A. Mom shopping for a shelf.   B. Dad and I fixing a shelf together.   C. Dad teaching me how to use a hammer.   D. The family cleaning the living room.
   ✅ B · 短文主要讲爸爸和“我”一起修架子，选项B最准确。

**第 9 篇**

> Today is our school sports day. I am in the 100-meter race. My friend Tom is in the long jump. He jumps very far. But suddenly he falls and hurts his leg. Our teacher Ms. Lee runs to help him. Tom says, "I am fine. I can try again." Everyone cheers for him.

**10.** What is the passage mainly about?
   A. A happy ending   B. A race and a jump   C. A teacher's advice   D. A fall and a friend's courage
   ✅ D · 短文主要讲汤姆摔倒后勇敢再试，体现勇气。

**第 10 篇**

> Hi everyone! Our school's Eco-Robot Club is looking for new members. We build robots from recycled materials. Last month, we made a robot bird that can plant seeds. No experience is needed — just bring your ideas. We meet every Friday at 3 p.m. in Room 201. Come and join us!

**11.** What special thing did the club do last month?
   A. They collected recycled materials.   B. They built a robot bird that can fly.   C. They planted trees in the park.   D. They made a robot that can plant seeds.
   ✅ D · 文中提到上个月做了能播种的机器鸟。

**第 11 篇**

> Hi, I’m Tom. Today I want to show you my new smart water bottle. It has a small screen that tells you how much water you drink. If you forget to drink, it plays a soft sound. It even lights up at night so you can find it easily. My mom says it helps me stay healthy.

**12.** What is the main purpose of this smart water bottle?
   A. To show the time   B. To keep water cold   C. To help people drink enough water   D. To play music at night
   ✅ C · 主旨题：短文强调提醒喝水、显示喝水量，目的是帮助健康饮水。

**第 12 篇**

> Emma felt a sharp pain in her right foot after stepping on a toy car at home. Her mom took her to the clinic. The doctor, Nurse Lee, didn't find any broken bones. He gave Emma a small note and said, "Please show this to your teacher tomorrow." Emma smiled—it was a note saying she could rest during PE class.

**13.** How did Emma hurt her foot?
   A. She stepped on a toy car.   B. She fell off the bed.   C. She ran too fast on the street.   D. She wore wrong shoes.
   ✅ A · 细节：文中明确说 stepping on a toy car at home。

**第 13 篇**

> Last Saturday, I went to a small film festival with my cousin. We watched a short movie about a boy who built a spaceship from cardboard. The best part was when the spaceship actually flew in the film! Later, we met the young director. He told us he made the movie in his garage. I felt really inspired.

**14.** How did the speaker feel after meeting the director?
   A. Worried.   B. Inspired.   C. Bored.   D. Surprised.
   ✅ B · 最后一句‘I felt really inspired’直接给出答案。

**第 14 篇**

> Mom and I are making fruit ice pops today. First, we cut strawberries and kiwis into small pieces. Then, we put them into the ice pop molds. Next, we pour some yogurt over the fruits. Finally, we put the molds into the freezer. We have to wait for six hours. I can’t wait to try them!

**15.** How long do they need to wait before eating?
   A. Four hours.   B. Two hours.   C. Six hours.   D. Eight hours.
   ✅ C · 文中提到‘wait for six hours’才能吃。
