## 听力 · Listening（30 分，20 题/项）

**1.** What is the main purpose of the activity?
   A. To clean up the beach   B. To learn about sea turtles   C. To collect old nets   D. To have fun on the beach
   ✅ A · 短文开头明确说参加海滩清理，这是主旨。

   ✅ D · 推断题，文中说“built a tiny robot that could draw pictures”，可知项目与机器人画画有关。

**4.** Why is the speaker a little worried?
   A. The apartment is far from the station   B. The landlord is unfriendly   C. The stairs in the building are narrow   D. The moving truck is late
   ✅ C · 推断题：原文提到“worried about the narrow stairs”，担心搬家具困难。
